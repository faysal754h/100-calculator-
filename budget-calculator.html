How to Use the Budget Calculator

  1. Enter your total income in USD (e.g., 5000) in the first input box.
  2. Enter your expenses in USD, separated by commas (e.g., 1000, 500, 200) in the second input box.
  3. Click the "Calculate Budget" button to see your budget summary.
  4. After every 3 uses, view a short ad to continue using the calculator.
