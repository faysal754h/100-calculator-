How to Use the Tip Calculator

  1. Enter the bill amount in dollars.
  2. Enter the desired tip percentage.
  3. Optionally, enter the number of people to split the bill.
  4. Click the "Calculate" button to see the tip amount, total bill, and per-person amounts (if applicable).
  5. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the calculator.
