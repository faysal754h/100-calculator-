How to Use the Party Food Calculator

  1. Enter the number of guests and event duration (in hours).
  2. Add food and drink items, specifying type, servings per guest, and optional cost per serving.
  3. Click "Calculate" to see total food/drink quantities and costs (if provided).
  4. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the calculator.
