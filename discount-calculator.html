How to Use the Discount Calculator

  1. Select a discount calculation (e.g., discounted price, discount amount, original price).
  2. Enter the required inputs (e.g., original price, discount percentage, discounted price, optional tax rate).
  3. Click the "Calculate" button to see your result.
  4. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the calculator.
