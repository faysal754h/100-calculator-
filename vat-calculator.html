How to Use the VAT Calculator

  1. Select a VAT calculation (e.g., VAT amount, net price, gross price).
  2. Enter the price, VAT rate, and select whether the price is net (excluding VAT) or gross (including VAT).
  3. Click the "Calculate" button to see your result.
  4. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the calculator.
