How to Use the Energy Usage Calculator

  1. Enter the energy price per kilowatt-hour (kWh) in your area (check your electricity bill for this value).
  2. Add appliances by entering their name, power consumption in watts (check appliance labels), and daily usage hours.
  3. Use the "Add Appliance" button to include more appliances as needed.
  4. Click "Calculate Energy Usage" to see daily, monthly, and yearly consumption and costs.
  5. Download the results as a text file for reference.
  6. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the calculator.
