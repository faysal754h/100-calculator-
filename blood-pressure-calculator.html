How to Use the Blood Pressure Calculator

  1. Enter your systolic blood pressure (e.g., 120 mmHg) in the first input box.
  2. Enter your diastolic blood pressure (e.g., 80 mmHg) in the second input box.
  3. Click the "Calculate BP Status" button to see your blood pressure status.
  4. After every 3 uses, view a short ad to continue using the calculator.
