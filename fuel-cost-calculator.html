How to Use the Fuel Cost Calculator

  1. Select a fuel cost calculation (e.g., total trip cost, fuel needed, cost per mile/km).
  2. Enter the distance, fuel efficiency, fuel price, and select the unit (miles/gallon or km/liter).
  3. Click the "Calculate" button to see your result.
  4. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the calculator.
