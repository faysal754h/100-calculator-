How to Use the Heart Rate Calculator

  1. Find your pulse on your wrist or neck.
  2. Count the number of beats for 15 seconds.
  3. Enter the number of beats in the input field.
  4. Click "Calculate Heart Rate" to see your beats per minute (bpm).
