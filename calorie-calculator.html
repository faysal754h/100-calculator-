How to Use the Calorie Calculator

  1. Select your gender from the dropdown menu.
  2. Enter your weight in kilograms (e.g., 70 kg).
  3. Enter your height in centimeters (e.g., 170 cm).
  4. Enter your age in years (e.g., 30).
  5. Select your activity level from the dropdown menu.
  6. Click the "Calculate Calories" button to see your daily calorie needs.
  7. After every 3 uses, view a short ad to continue using the calculator.
