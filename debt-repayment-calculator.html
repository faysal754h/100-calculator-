How to Use the Debt Repayment Calculator

  1. Enter the total loan amount in USD (e.g., 10000).
  2. Enter the annual interest rate as a percentage (e.g., 5).
  3. Enter the loan term in years (e.g., 5).
  4. Click the "Calculate Repayment" button to see your monthly payment, total interest, and total repayment.
  5. After every 3 uses, view a short ad to continue using the calculator.
