How to Use the Venue Capacity Calculator

  1. Enter the venue's usable area in square feet.
  2. Select an event type/layout or choose "Custom" to specify space per person.
  3. Optionally, enter a safety reduction percentage for fire codes or comfort.
  4. Click "Calculate" to see the estimated maximum guest capacity.
  5. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the calculator.
