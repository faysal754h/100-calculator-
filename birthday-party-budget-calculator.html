How to Use the Birthday Party Budget Calculator

  1. Enter the number of guests for your birthday party.
  2. Input costs (e.g., food, drinks, venue, decorations, entertainment).
  3. Optionally, add budget categories and their percentage allocations (e.g., 30% for food).
  4. Click "Calculate" to see the total cost, per-guest cost, and budget breakdown.
  5. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the calculator.
