How to Use the Vacation Days Calculator

  1. Enter the start and end dates of your vacation period.
  2. Input your total allocated vacation days.
  3. Check "Exclude Weekends" if desired, and enter the number of public holidays or specific exclusion dates.
  4. Optionally, input daily costs for accommodations, food, and activities to estimate your budget.
  5. Click "Calculate" to see usable vacation days and total estimated costs.
  6. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the calculator.
