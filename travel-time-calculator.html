How to Use the Travel Time Calculator

  1. Select a travel calculation (e.g., total travel time, arrival time, required speed).
  2. Enter the required inputs (e.g., distance, speed, departure time, stops, unit).
  3. Click the "Calculate" button to see your result.
  4. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the calculator.
