How to Use the Trigonometry Calculator

  1. Select the trigonometric function (sin, cos, tan, or their inverses).
  2. Enter an angle in degrees for sin, cos, or tan, or a ratio value for arcsin, arccos, or arctan.
  3. Click the "Calculate" button to see your result.
  4. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the calculator.
