How to Use the BMI Calculator

  1. Enter your weight in kilograms (e.g., 70 kg) in the first input box.
  2. Enter your height in centimeters (e.g., 170 cm) in the second input box.
  3. Click the "Calculate BMI" button to see your BMI and health status.
  4. After every 3 uses, view a short ad to continue using the calculator.
