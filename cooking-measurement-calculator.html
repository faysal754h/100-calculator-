How to Use the Cooking Measurement Converter

  1. Select an operation: Convert Measurement or Scale Recipe.
  2. For conversions, enter the amount and select the from/to units.
  3. For scaling, add ingredients with amounts and units, then specify original and new servings.
  4. Click "Calculate" to see the result.
  5. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the converter.
