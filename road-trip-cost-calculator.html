How to Use the Road Trip Cost Calculator

  1. Enter the total distance of your trip (in miles or km).
  2. Input your vehicle's fuel efficiency and fuel price per gallon or liter.
  3. Specify the number of travelers and costs (e.g., accommodations, food, additional expenses as a percentage).
  4. Click "Calculate" to see the total cost, per-person cost, and estimated fuel consumption.
  5. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the calculator.
