How to Use the Geometry Calculator

  1. Select a geometry calculation (e.g., triangle area, cylinder volume).
  2. Enter the required inputs based on the calculation (e.g., base, height, radius).
  3. Click the "Calculate" button to see your result.
  4. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the calculator.
