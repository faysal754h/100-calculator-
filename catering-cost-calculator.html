How to Use the Catering Cost Calculator

  1. Enter the number of guests for your event.
  2. Input costs (e.g., food, drinks, service fee) and optional gratuity/tax percentages.
  3. Specify food portions and drink servings per guest for quantity estimates.
  4. Click "Calculate" to see the total cost, per-guest cost, and estimated quantities.
  5. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the calculator.
