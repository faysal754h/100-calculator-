How to Use the CGPA Calculator

  1. Enter the credit hours for a course (e.g., 3).
  2. Select the grade you received for that course (e.g., A, B+).
  3. Click "Add Another Course" to include more courses if needed.
  4. Click the "Calculate CGPA" button to see your Cumulative Grade Point Average.
  5. After every 3 uses, view a short ad to continue using the calculator.
