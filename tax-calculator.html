How the Tax Calculator Works

This calculator uses a simplified tiered tax system:

  • 10% for the first $10,000
  • 20% for income between $10,001 and $40,000
  • 30% for income over $40,000

It assumes standard deductions and does not account for state taxes or special credits. For exact tax filing, consult a certified tax advisor.
