How to Use the Shopping List Calculator

  1. Add items to your shopping list by entering the item name, price, and quantity.
  2. Click "Add Item" to include more items, or remove items as needed.
  3. Enter an optional discount percentage and tax rate.
  4. Click "Calculate" to see the total cost, savings, and itemized list.
  5. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the calculator.
