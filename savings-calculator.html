How to Use the Savings Calculator

  1. Enter the initial amount you are starting with.
  2. Enter your monthly contribution amount.
  3. Enter the annual interest rate and time period in years.
  4. Click the "Calculate Savings" button to see your results.
  5. After 3 calculations, you will be redirected to an ad. Return to continue using the calculator.
